Namecoin is our 1st Smart Contract on Etherparty

Creating a Smart Contract requires verified names, Etherparty makes this easy with Namecoin. We leverage a decentralized name registry system so you can track who you're making agreements with. Namecoin on Etherparty manages names, aliases, emails, and your registration number so you can prove that you're ready to be a party to a Smart Contract. Try Smart Contracts today with Etherparty!
